Welcome to CO2 Metrics!

Here you can find the information about the CO2 emissions by different countries across various industries.

Every year, tonnes of Carbondioxide is released in to the atmosphere due to various activities of mankind.
This carbondioxide traps heat, increasing the average temparature of the earth. This has been causing adverse effects such as melting of ice bergs, raising the sea levels etc.
This website provides a visualization of the carbondioxide emissions from 45 countries across different industries.
